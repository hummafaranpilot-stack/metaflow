Key Ingredients

BHB Ketone Blend
Read more

Beta-Hydroxybutyrate (BHB) is an exogenous ketone that helps your body enter ketosis faster. It provides an immediate source of clean energy for your brain and muscles.

Studies show BHB can help reduce appetite, increase mental clarity, and accelerate fat burning — even when you're not on a strict ketogenic diet. The blend of Calcium, Magnesium, and Sodium BHB works synergistically to maximize ketone levels and support optimal hydration during ketosis.

MCT Oil Powder
Read more

MCT Oil Powder is made from Medium Chain Triglycerides sourced from pure coconut oil. Unlike regular fats, MCTs are rapidly absorbed and instantly converted into ketones by the liver.

Findings show MCT Oil can provide immediate brain fuel, boost energy levels, enhance mental clarity, and accelerate fat burning without the crash of caffeine or sugar.

Irish Sea Moss
Read more

Irish Sea Moss (Chondrus crispus) is a red seaweed found on the rocky coasts of Ireland, packed with 92 essential minerals your body needs every day. It naturally provides calcium, iron, potassium, magnesium, zinc, and copper.

During the Great Irish Famine, communities survived by eating this nutritious seaweed. Studies show Irish Sea Moss supports thyroid health, gut health, heart health, and replenishes electrolytes lost during ketosis.

Apple Cider Vinegar
Read more

Apple Cider Vinegar (ACV) has been used for centuries as a traditional remedy to support healthy digestion and balanced blood sugar levels. It's made by fermenting crushed apples until the natural sugars turn into acetic acid.

Findings show ACV can promote a feeling of fullness, reduce cravings, support healthy weight management, and improve gut health. It's one of the most trusted natural ingredients in modern wellness.

Green Tea Extract
Read more

Green Tea Extract is derived from the leaves of Camellia sinensis, traditionally harvested in the mountains of China and Japan. It's rich in powerful antioxidants and EGCG (epigallocatechin gallate).

Studies show Green Tea Extract can boost metabolism, enhance fat oxidation, and provide a gentle sustained energy boost without the jitters associated with high-caffeine products. It's one of the most studied natural fat-burning compounds in the world.

BioPerine
Read more

BioPerine is a patented black pepper extract standardized to 95% Piperine, harvested from Piper nigrum fruit grown along the Malabar Coast of Southern India.

Findings show BioPerine enhances the bioavailability and absorption of other nutrients by up to 30%, making every ingredient in the formula work significantly harder. It also supports healthy digestion and nutrient uptake.
